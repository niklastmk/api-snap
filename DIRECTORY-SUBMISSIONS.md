## Quick Manual Submission Guide (SnapQR)

Use these details when submitting to directories that require account login:

- **Product name:** SnapQR
- **URL:** https://api-snap.com/snapqr
- **Tagline:** Free QR codes with real-time scan analytics — see who scanned, where, when
- **Description:** SnapQR generates trackable QR codes for free — no signup needed. Every scan is logged with device, location, and time data. View your analytics dashboard at a shareable link. Upgrade to Pro ($7/mo) for unlimited codes, clean images, CSV export, and 30-day history.
- **Category:** QR Code tools / URL shorteners
- **Alternatives to:** Bitly, QR Tiger, QR Code Generator

### Per-site instructions (~2 min each):

**AlternativeTo.net** — Create account → search "QR Tiger" or "Bitly" → click "Suggest Alternative" → enter SnapQR details above.

**BetaList.com** — Sign in (email or Twitter) → Submit Startup → fill name, URL, tagline, description.

**Fazier.com** — Visit fazier.com/submit → create account if needed → fill product details → choose free tier ("Lite").

**SaaSHub.com** — Register → Add product → verify ownership → go to management page → Submit tab → post to directories. SaaSHub also has a built-in tool to submit to 108+ other directories from their dashboard.
